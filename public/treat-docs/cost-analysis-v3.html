Synthesis & CMC Reality

This is specialty synthesis (semaglutide-class complexity) — not commodity, not biologic.

Peptide Synthesis (Stage 2): $60K–$160K

  • 39-AA lipidated peptide = semaglutide-class
  • Per-peptide cost: $6K–$12K at mid-tier CROs
  • C20 fatty diacid + γGlu-γGlu linker is the cost driver ($2K–$5K custom)
  • 20–40% synthesis failure rate for full lipidated 39-mer
  • Budget: 5 candidates + 3–4 backup syntheses

CMC / Manufacturing (Stage 7): $700K–$1.8M

GMP process dev + tech transfer$80K–$200K
Analytical method dev + validation$100K–$220K
Primary GMP batch (100–500g)$100K–$400K
Process validation (3 batches)$100K–$400K
Formulation + fill-finish + stability$123K–$275K
Deep review was too high ($2–5M): Applied biologic/mAb-grade costs to a synthetic peptide
v2 was too low ($350K): Missed process validation, formulation complexity, analytical scope

Regulatory Pathway

CNADA Optimistic

  • Requires: "serious/life-threatening or unmet need" + "complex/difficult effectiveness studies"
  • Eligibility is NOT guaranteed — go/no-go gate at Month 3–4 CVM meeting
  • Effectiveness standard: "reasonable expectation" (pilot study sufficient)
  • ADUFA fee: ~$354K (supplemental) to $709K (full)
  • Timeline: 24–28 months

Full NADA Base Case

  • "Substantial evidence of effectiveness" = pivotal field study required
  • 120–250 dogs, 8–15 sites, $1.5M–$3.8M additional
  • ADUFA NADA fee: $708,863 (FY2026 confirmed)
  • Timeline: 36–42 months

GLP Target Animal Safety — The Biggest Variable

Study Design (VICH GL43 / CVM GFI #185)

  • 4 groups: control + 1×/3×/5× dose
  • 8 dogs/group (4M + 4F) + 8 recovery = 40 dogs total
  • GLP-compliant (21 CFR Part 58)

Base Case: 6-Month Study $750K–$1.8M

  • Chronic-use drug → CVM default is 6 months
  • Housing: $168K–$336K
  • TK sampling: $120K–$360K
  • Drug supply: $60K–$150K

Optimistic: 90-Day Study Saves $150K–$500K

  • Only if CVM agrees based on strong PK data (no accumulation)
  • Housing savings: $84K–$168K
  • Also saves ~3 months on timeline
  • Treat 90 days as upside, not the plan
CVM decides the duration — early INAD engagement is critical.
File INAD in Month 1, pre-submission meeting by Month 3–4.

Clinical Strategy

Pilot Field Study (Stage 6) $450K–$1.1M

  • 20–40 client-owned obese dogs
  • 3–5 vet clinics/schools, 4–6 months enrollment
  • Endpoints: weight loss, BCS, metabolic markers, DEXA subset
  • Serves as CNADA "reasonable expectation of effectiveness" (RXE)
  • Designed to serve both pathways — dose-ranging data for pivotal if needed

Pivotal Field Study (Stage 10) $1.5M–$3.8M

  • 120–250 client-owned obese dogs
  • 8–15 sites, 6–8 months enrollment
  • "Substantial evidence of effectiveness" (SEE) standard
  • Site PI fees: $300K–$750K
  • Vet visits: $200K–$500K
  • Drug supply: $200K–$500K
