Thesis

Human GLP-1 drugs proved the category. Treat builds the canine version.

Semaglutide and retatrutide created a $100B+ human obesity market. The same receptor biology exists in dogs. Treat is the first company building a canine-specific peptide platform to capture that opportunity.

Pipeline

Three programs spanning fat loss, lean-mass preservation, and body recomposition.

TRT-001

Triple incretin agonist

GLP-1R/GIPR/GCGR. Canine-optimized from a retatrutide-class scaffold.

Indication: Canine obesity and metabolic health

Lead optimization
TRT-002

Engineered follistatin-derived peptide

Dual myostatin and activin antagonism for lean-mass support.

Indication: Lean-mass preservation during weight loss

Discovery
TRT-003

Combination program

Indication: Body recomposition
