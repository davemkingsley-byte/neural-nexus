Objective

Develop TRT-001, the first canine-optimized triple incretin agonist targeting GLP-1R, GIPR, and GCGR simultaneously for canine obesity. Validate computationally, confirm in vitro binding at all three canine receptors, and position for FDA CVM conditional approval (CNADA). Pipeline v2: ActRIIB blockade for muscle preservation during weight loss.

The Play: 60% of US dogs (54 million) are overweight. Zero approved treatments since 2010. Retatrutide (Eli Lilly) achieved 24.2% weight loss in humans — the highest ever recorded. We're building the canine-optimized version. Lilly is spending $100M+ validating the exact combination (incretin + ActRII) in humans. We ride the validation wave into the veterinary market.
