Objective

Restart Treat Biosciences with a pivot from monoclonal antibodies to AI-designed peptide therapeutics targeting canine obesity (GLP-1 + ActRII combination). Validate the thesis computationally, confirm in vitro binding, and position for accelerator/SBIR funding.

Key Science

Canine GLP-1R Identity90.7% identical to human (41 substitutions / 441 residues)
Canine ActRIIB Identity99.8% identical to human (1 substitution / 495 residues)
CompetitionZERO — no canine GLP-1 or ActRII therapeutics exist
Human ValidationEli Lilly Phase 2 trial (NCT06643728) — bimagrumab + tirzepatide, 240 patients
Canine PoCDik et al. 2025 — liraglutide achieved 13% weight loss in obese dogs
Patent LandscapeFTO clear — no blocking patents for canine peptide combination
