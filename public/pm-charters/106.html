Objective
Automated pipeline that converts Neural NeXus Substack posts into TikTok-ready short-form video content, designed to drive traffic back to Substack for subscriber growth.
Success Criteria
One Substack post → multiple TikTok-ready clips (30-90s), with hooks, captions, CTA overlay pointing to Substack. Minimal manual effort per post.
Sign-Off
🟡 In Progress — David Kingsley · 2026-03-16
✅ In Scope
  • Parse Substack post (URL or RSS) into structured sections
  • Extract 3-5 hook-worthy segments per post
  • Generate TikTok scripts (hook → context → payoff → CTA)
  • Auto-generate burned-in captions (TikTok style)
  • Add CTA overlay: Subscribe link
  • Text-to-speech narration (AI voice / David's voice clone)
  • 9:16 vertical format, 30-90 seconds per clip
  • Neural NeXus logo watermark, branding
  • Export as MP4 ready for TikTok upload
  • Batch mode: one post → 3-5 clips
🚫 Out of Scope
  • General-purpose video production suite
  • Non-Substack content sources
  • Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts (for now)
