Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

Overview

Neural NeXus ("we," "our") operates the website neuralnexus.press and associated tools, including the Cognitive Performance Dashboard. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you use our cognitive testing tools or connect third-party services, we may collect:

  • Cognitive test results: Scores, response times, and session data from tests you voluntarily take (N-Back, PVT, DSST)
  • Chess.com data: Publicly available rating information from the Chess.com API
  • WHOOP data: If you authorize our application, we access sleep metrics, recovery scores, heart rate variability, and related biometric data through the WHOOP API. We only request read access to sleep, recovery, and cycle data.
  • Self-reported data: Subjective ratings (energy, focus, sleep hours) that you voluntarily provide
  • Supplement tracking: Supplement names and start dates that you voluntarily enter

How We Use Your Information

  • To display your personal cognitive performance trends and analytics
  • To correlate biometric data with cognitive test results for your personal insight
  • To track longitudinal changes in cognitive performance

We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal data to any third parties.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a password-protected database on our servers. Access to the cognitive dashboard requires authentication. WHOOP API tokens are stored securely and are never exposed to the client.

Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following services:

  • WHOOP: We access your data via the WHOOP Developer API using OAuth 2.0. You can revoke access at any time through your WHOOP app settings.
  • Chess.com: We access publicly available player statistics through the Chess.com Public API.
  • Google Analytics: We use GA4 for anonymous website usage analytics.

Data Retention

Your cognitive test data and biometric data are retained indefinitely for longitudinal tracking purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

  • You can disconnect WHOOP access at any time via the WHOOP app
  • You can request export of all your data (CSV or JSON) from the dashboard
  • You can request deletion of your data by contacting us
